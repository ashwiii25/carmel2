Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU)

Welcome to Carmel Hospital’s Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU)! Our team of pediatric specialists is dedicated to providing expert and compassionate care to critically ill children. We understand that the care of sick children requires specialized knowledge and expertise, and we are committed to providing the highest quality of care to each child.

Our PICU is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and resources to provide a full range of critical care services, including monitoring, diagnosis, and treatment of complex medical conditions. Our team includes pediatric intensivists, nurses, respiratory therapists, and other specialists who work together to provide comprehensive care to our patients.

At Carmel Hospital’s PICU, we understand that the wellbeing of our patients’ families and loved ones is just as important as the wellbeing of our patients. That’s why we offer support and resources to families and loved ones of our patients, including education on critical care, updates on their child’s condition, and emotional support.

We know that being in the PICU can be a difficult and stressful experience, which is why we strive to create a calm and supportive environment for our patients and their families. Our team is committed to providing personalized care and support to each child and their family throughout their time in the PICU.

Thank you for choosing Carmel Hospital’s PICU for your child’s critical care needs. We are dedicated to providing expert and compassionate care to our patients, and we are committed to helping them achieve the best possible outcome.
