Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU)

Welcome to Carmel Hospital’s Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU)! Our team of neonatal specialists is dedicated to providing expert and compassionate care to premature and critically ill newborns. We understand that caring for premature and critically ill newborns requires specialized knowledge and expertise, and we are committed to providing the highest quality of care to each baby.

Our NICU is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and resources to provide a full range of neonatal care services, including monitoring, diagnosis, and treatment of complex medical conditions. Our team includes neonatologists, nurses, respiratory therapists, and other specialists who work together to provide comprehensive care to our patients.

At Carmel Hospital’s NICU, we understand that the wellbeing of our patients’ families and loved ones is just as important as the wellbeing of our patients. That’s why we offer support and resources to families and loved ones of our patients, including education on neonatal care, updates on their baby’s condition, and emotional support.

We know that having a premature or critically ill newborn in the NICU can be a difficult and stressful experience, which is why we strive to create a calm and supportive environment for our patients and their families. Our team is committed to providing personalized care and support to each baby and their family throughout their time in the NICU.

Thank you for choosing Carmel Hospital’s NICU for your newborn’s care needs. We are dedicated to providing expert and compassionate care to our patients, and we are committed to helping them achieve the best possible outcome.
