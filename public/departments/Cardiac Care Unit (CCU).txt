Cardiac Care Unit (CCU)

Welcome to Carmel Hospital’s Cardiac Care Unit (CCU)! Our team of cardiac specialists is dedicated to providing expert and compassionate care to patients with heart conditions. We understand that cardiac care requires specialized knowledge and expertise, and we are committed to providing the highest quality of care to each patient.

Our CCU is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and resources to provide a full range of cardiac care services, including monitoring, diagnosis, and treatment of complex heart conditions. Our team includes cardiologists, nurses, and other specialists who work together to provide comprehensive care to our patients.

At Carmel Hospital’s CCU, we understand that the wellbeing of our patients’ families and loved ones is just as important as the wellbeing of our patients. That’s why we offer support and resources to families and loved ones of our patients, including education on cardiac care, updates on their loved one’s condition, and emotional support.

We know that being in the CCU can be a difficult and stressful experience, which is why we strive to create a calm and supportive environment for our patients and their families. Our team is committed to providing personalized care and support to each patient and their family throughout their time in the CCU.

Thank you for choosing Carmel Hospital’s CCU for your cardiac care needs. We are dedicated to providing expert and compassionate care to our patients, and we are committed to helping them achieve the best possible outcome.
