Medical Intensive Care Unit (MICU)

Welcome to Carmel Hospital’s Medical Intensive Care Unit (MICU)! Our team of highly skilled and experienced healthcare professionals is dedicated to providing compassionate and advanced care to critically ill patients. We understand that patients in the MICU require specialized care and attention, and we are committed to providing the highest quality of care to each patient.

Our MICU is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and resources to provide a full range of critical care services, including monitoring, diagnosis, and treatment of complex medical conditions. Our team of experts includes intensivists, nurses, respiratory therapists, and other specialists who work together to provide comprehensive care to our patients.

At Carmel Hospital’s MICU, we understand that the wellbeing of our patients’ families and loved ones is just as important as the wellbeing of our patients. That’s why we offer support and resources to families and loved ones of our patients, including education on critical care, updates on their loved one’s condition, and emotional support.

We know that being in the MICU can be a difficult and stressful experience, which is why we strive to create a calm and supportive environment for our patients and their families. Our team is committed to providing personalized care and support to each patient and their family throughout their time in the MICU.

Thank you for choosing Carmel Hospital’s MICU for your critical care needs. We are dedicated to providing advanced and compassionate care to our patients, and we are committed to helping them achieve the best possible outcome.
