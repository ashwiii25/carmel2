Anaesthesia department

Welcome to the Anaesthesia department at Carmel Hospital.
Our highly skilled team of anaesthesiologists and anaesthesia technicians are dedicated to providing safe and effective care to our patients.

We understand that surgery and other procedures can be stressful for patients, which is why we strive to make the anaesthesia process as comfortable and stress-free as possible. Our team uses the latest technologies and techniques to provide a wide range of anaesthesia services, from general anaesthesia to regional anaesthesia and conscious sedation.

Our anaesthesiologists work closely with other healthcare professionals to create a customized anaesthesia plan for each patient, taking into consideration their medical history, medications, and other factors. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that our patients are comfortable and safe throughout their procedure.

At Carmel Hospital’s Anaesthesia department, we prioritize patient safety and follow strict safety protocols and guidelines. We utilize the latest monitoring technologies to ensure that our patients are closely monitored during their procedure, and our team is trained to quickly respond to any complications or emergencies that may arise.

We are committed to providing exceptional care and service to our patients. Thank you for considering Carmel Hospital’s Anaesthesia department for your healthcare needs.
