General Surgery department

Welcome to the General Surgery department at Carmel Hospital. Our team of experienced general surgeons and healthcare professionals are dedicated to providing high-quality surgical care to our patients.

Our general surgeons have extensive training and experience in performing a wide range of surgical procedures, including laparoscopic and minimally invasive surgeries. We utilize the latest technologies and techniques to ensure that our patients receive safe, effective, and timely care.

At Carmel Hospital’s General Surgery department, we treat a variety of conditions, including appendicitis, gallstones, hernias, and cancers of the digestive system. We also provide emergency surgical services for patients in critical condition.

We understand that undergoing surgery can be a stressful and uncertain time for patients and their families, which is why we prioritize open communication with our patients to ensure they understand their condition, treatment options, and what to expect throughout their surgical experience.

Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional care and service to our patients, and we are committed to ensuring that our patients have a comfortable and positive experience during their time with us. Thank you for considering Carmel Hospital’s General Surgery department for your healthcare needs.
