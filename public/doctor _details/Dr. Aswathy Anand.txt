Dr. Aswathy Anand


Dr. Aswathy Anand is a highly qualified and experienced clinical psychologist with an M.Phil and PhD in the field of psychology, as well as a PGCFM qualification. With over a decade of experience in the field of psychological assessment, adolescent health and wellness, family counselling, and learning assessment and remediation, Dr. Anand is a leading expert in her field.

She began her career at Alphavista Health Care in Kakkanad, where she gained valuable experience working with patients and developing her skills as a clinician. Later, Later she joined at life skill counseling centre kollam, currently she is providing her service to Najath hospital , Aluva and sound waves clinic , Aluva. where she continued to provide compassionate and effective care to patients in need.

Throughout her career, Dr. Anand has developed a reputation for her expertise in adolescent health and wellness, family counselling, and learning assessment and remediation. She is committed to helping her patients achieve optimal mental health and wellbeing, and she works closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that address their unique needs and challenges.

With her extensive experience and advanced qualifications, Dr. Aswathy Anand is a respected leader in the field of clinical psychology. She continues to pursue ongoing professional development opportunities to ensure that she remains at the forefront of her field and provides her patients with the best possible care.
